## 📘 Project Overview

* This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior to understand retention and churn patterns.
* Using a dashboard-driven approach, the analysis explores how factors such as age group, tenure, payment method, and contract type influence customer loyalty and revenue generation.
* The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer distribution, revenue contribution, and churn dynamics.


## 📁 Dataset Description

* The dataset contains customer-level information including 
> customer ID, age, gender, tenure, monthly charges, contract type, payment method, total charges, and churn status.

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Customer distribution shows that middle-aged customers form the largest segment.
* Tenure analysis indicates that most customers are in mid-term and long-term categories.
* Churn is higher among customers with shorter tenure, showing early-stage drop-off.
* Month-to-month contracts have higher churn compared to long-term contracts.
* Payment method analysis reveals that electronic check users have higher churn, while credit card and bank transfer users show better retention.
* Retained customers contribute more revenue due to longer tenure.

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Out of 100K customers, about 33% have churned, indicating a high churn rate.
* 292.61M revenue is mainly driven by retained and long-tenure customers.
* Customers with short tenure and month-to-month contracts are more likely to churn.
* Electronic check users show higher churn, while credit card and bank transfer users are more stable.
* Younger customers have relatively higher churn compared to other age groups.
* Long-term contracts and higher tenure strongly correlate with better retention and revenue. 

## 💡 Recommendations

* Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts through discounts and offers.
* Improve early customer engagement to reduce churn in the initial months.
* Promote secure payment methods like credit card and bank transfer over electronic checks Target high-risk groups (young and short-tenure customers) with personalized retention strategies.
* Introduce loyalty programs and feedback systems to improve long-term retention



## ▶️ How to Use

* Open the dashboard in Power BI Desktop to explore customer data.
* Use slicers to filter by gender, contract type, payment method, and tenure group.
* Interact with charts to understand churn patterns and customer distribution.
* Hover over visuals to view detailed insights on retention and churn.
* Analyze customer segments to identify high-risk groups and retention opportunities.
* Use the dashboard to gain insights into customer behavior and improve decision-making.
